# Module 7: Wireless Networking

## 7.1 Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11)
**Standards and speeds:**
- **802.11a**: 54 Mbps (5 GHz)
- **802.11b**: 11 Mbps (2.4 GHz)
- **802.11g**: 54 Mbps (2.4 GHz)
- **802.11n**: 600 Mbps (2.4/5 GHz) — Wi-Fi 4
- **802.11ac**: 1 Gbps+ (5 GHz) — Wi-Fi 5
- **802.11ax**: 9.6 Gbps (2.4/5/6 GHz) — Wi-Fi 6

**2.4 GHz vs 5 GHz:**
- **2.4 GHz**: Longer range, more interference, slower
- **5 GHz**: Shorter range, less interference, faster

## 7.2 BLUETOOTH
- Short-range wireless (PAN, ~10m)
- Used for: Headphones, keyboards, mice, IoT sensors
